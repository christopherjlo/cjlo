Favorite Foods

I like all types of food and am not picky, but I like rice dishes the most. I could eat fried rice every day! My favorite noodle dish is probably ramen. I usually get tonkotsu (pork broth) ramen, and lately I've started to like putting more chilis in them to make it spicier. My spice tolerance isn't great, but I'm trying to get better at eating spicy food.
